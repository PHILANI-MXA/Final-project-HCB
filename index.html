About

Brief overview

My passion for technology started when I was in high school. I couldn't get a place to study at the institutions of higher learning because I didn't have Maths. I was told that I did not meet the minimum requirements. There weren't any formal assessments that were put in place in order to check the ability of the applicants. I am a young, energetic individual who enjoys learning new things, especially things that are challenging. I like to challenge people in mind games just to test my level of ability at a particular thing. Because of my mood swings, I can't describe whether I'm an introverted or extrovert. Sometimes I like to talk and be out there, and sometimes I like to enjoy my space alone. I don't have any emotional stress; it's just how I am.
