About

Brief overview

My name is Philani, I am from Philiipi. I matriculated in 2017, in 2018 because of few employment opportunities in our country I have then decided to go for a (1 year contract) Premier's Advancement of Youth (PAY) internship by the Western Cape Government at the department of Cultural Affairs and Sport. I went there with the hopes of gaining a valuable work experience and I was aware that the South African government system requires work experience even if you have a qualification but without experience it becomes difficult for one to get employed. I was placed as Administration clerk Human Resource Support, where I was particular dealing with internal HR functions of the department. I like to challenge people in mind games just to test my level of ability at a particular thing.
